◆ ks_release()

char* ks_release ( kstring_t *  ks)

release kstring_t

注解
Give ownership of underlying buffer away to something else (making that something else responsible for freeing it), leaving kstring_t empty and ready to be used again, or ready to go out of scope without needing free(str.s) to prevent a memory leak.
参数
[in]kspointer of kstring_t
返回
pointer of kstring_t string
